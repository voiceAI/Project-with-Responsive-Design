is Voice AI Search Engine Optimization (Voice SEO)?


Once a web site is optimized for the essentials of (SEO), Voice-based search engine optimization Voice SEO can be practiced. Voice SEO includes the process of using conversational artificial intelligence (AI), by vocally or manually submitting a question, fragments of words, and or request. Once the submission is queried, the conversational AI will provide the most accurate and dominant answer/result to the individual submitting the query. Voice SEO includes back-end SEO, machine learning, and conversational AI.
